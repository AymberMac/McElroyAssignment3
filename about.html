About Me

I am dedicated to a career preserving the landscapes and natural resources that make our diverse environments so precious. By blending conservation and resource planning with natural science processes through the lens of GIS analysis, the real impacts of land and water use can be determined. This data can support sustainable actions to help ensure healthy natural resources for present and future generations to enjoy.
